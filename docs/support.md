# Foundry Vox Support

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Foundry Vox support is intended to help with installation, generation,
playback, export, and cloning issues.

## Before you contact support

Please include:
- your macOS version
- the approximate time of the issue
- the selected voice
- the selected quality tier
- the export format you were using
- the support snapshot copied from the app, if available

## Common things to mention

- whether this happened on first launch or after the app had already warmed up
- whether the issue affected generation, playback, export, or cloning
- whether the issue was repeatable

## Rights and cloning

If your issue involves voice cloning, note whether you own or control the audio
used for the clone. Foundry Vox requires users to have the right to use the
recordings they upload.
